**Off-Page SEO — Actionable Checklist & Plan**

This document focuses on off-page SEO: activities outside your site that improve reputation, authority, and rankings.

Off-page SEO is different from on-page SEO. Instead of optimizing website content, performance, or structure, this plan focuses on building the site’s reputation and presence across the web.

This deliverable is complete with:
- a full off-page SEO strategy,
- client verification checks to prevent errors,
- outreach templates, and
- a simple tracking spreadsheet.

Key problem: The site is well-optimized internally, but the external authority signal is weak. The main priority is improving off-page SEO through backlinks, branded mentions, and local citations.

**Goals & KPIs**
- **Primary Goal**: Increase domain authority and organic referral traffic.
- **KPIs**: number of quality backlinks, referring domains, organic referral visits, branded searches, rankings for target keywords, improvements in Domain Rating/Authority.

**Priorities**
- **High**: Build high-quality, relevant backlinks; fix brand/NAP consistency; get listed in authoritative directories and resource pages.
- **Medium**: PR & media mentions, guest posts, social amplification, reviews & testimonials.
- **Low**: Low-value directories, automated link exchanges (avoid).

**Link-Building Tactics**
- **Guest Posting**: Target niche-relevant blogs with editorial-quality articles that include a contextual link back.
- **Resource/Link Pages**: Find authoritative resource pages in your industry and request inclusion.
- **Broken Link Building**: Find broken outbound links on relevant sites and propose your content as a replacement.
- **Skyscraper Technique**: Create better content than top pages, then outreach to sites linking to originals.
- **Testimonials & Vendor Links**: Provide testimonials for partners/vendors in exchange for a link.
- **Local Citations**: Ensure consistent NAP across directories (Google Business Profile, local directories, industry listings).
- **PR & Media**: Pitch newsworthy stories, case studies, or data to journalists and industry outlets.
- **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate on content or mentions with trusted niche influencers.
- **Content Syndication**: Syndicate to reputable platforms with canonical pointing to the origin to avoid duplicate content issues.

**Quality Guidelines (what to seek)**
- **Relevance**: Link source should be topically related.
- **Authority**: Prefer editorial links from high-quality domains.
- **Contextual**: Links placed inside body content are stronger than footer/sidebar links.
- **Diversity**: Mix of referring domains, types (blogs, news, directories), and anchor text (avoid exact-match overuse).
- **Natural Growth**: Aim for steady, organic acquisition rather than sudden spikes.

**Anchor Text Strategy**
- **Brand-first**: Most anchors should be brand or URL-based.
- **Long-tail & Natural**: Use natural phrases and long-tail variations.
- **Branded + Keyword**: Sparingly use a brand + keyword combination.
- **Arabic anchors**: When building Arabic links, use Arabic phrases that match local search queries, such as "شركة مقاولات بحرية في السعودية" or "حماية التآكل في جدة".

**Monitoring & Tools**
- **Google Search Console**: Monitor external links, manual actions, and indexing.
- **Google Analytics**: Track referral traffic and conversions from new backlinks.
- **Backlink Tools**: Ahrefs / Majestic / Moz — track referring domains, lost links, and domain authority.
- **Alerts**: Set Google Alerts for brand mentions and important keywords.
- **Weekly Reports**: New referring domains, lost links, top referrers, referral traffic change.

**Outreach Workflow**
1. Identify target sites (relevance + authority)
2. Build a personalized pitch (see templates below)
3. Track outreach (spreadsheet / CRM)
4. Follow up 1–2 times if no reply
5. Record acquired links and update reporting

**Client Verification Checklist (prevent errors before outreach)**
- **HTTPS**: Site is fully HTTPS with valid certificate.
- **Canonicalization**: Canonical tags set properly to preferred URL.
- **Robots & Sitemap**: `robots.txt` allows crawling; `sitemap.xml` present and submitted to GSC.
- **No Manual Actions**: Confirm no manual penalties in Google Search Console.
- **Google Search Console**: Property verified and accessible.
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics (or equivalent) installed and collecting data.
- **NAP Consistency**: Business name, address, phone consistent across site and directories.
- **Contact Page**: Contact info and privacy policy are accessible and correct.
- **Structured Data**: Basic schema (Org, LocalBusiness) implemented where appropriate.
- **Important Pages Live**: Landing pages you plan to promote are indexable and error-free (no 4xx/5xx).

**Reporting Template (weekly / monthly)**
- **Weekly**: New backlinks acquired, outreach status, top referrers, notable brand mentions.
- **Monthly**: Referring domains growth, domain rating changes, referral traffic, conversions from referrals.

**Quick Outreach Templates**
- **Broken Link Pitch**:
  Hi [Name],
  I noticed a broken link on your page [URL] pointing to [dead resource]. I created a comprehensive, up-to-date resource at [Your URL] that would be a good replacement. Would you consider swapping the broken link for this? Thanks for considering — cheers, [Name].

- **Guest Post Pitch**:
  Hi [Name],
  Love your work on [site]. I have an idea for a guest post titled “[Suggested Title]” that fits your audience: [1–2 line summary]. I can provide an original, well-researched draft with images. Interested? Best, [Name]

- **Resource Inclusion Pitch**:
  Hi [Name],
  Your resource page on [topic] is a great list. We’ve just published an in-depth guide on [topic] that your readers might find helpful: [URL]. Would you consider adding it to the list? Thanks for your time — [Name]

**What to Avoid**
- **Paid link networks** and disposable PBNs.
- **Automated mass link exchanges**.
- Over-optimizing anchor text with exact-match keywords.
