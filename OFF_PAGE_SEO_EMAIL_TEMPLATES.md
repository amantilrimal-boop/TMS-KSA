# Off-Page SEO Outreach Templates

Use these templates for link-building outreach and off-site SEO outreach. Customize each message to the recipient and site.

## 1. Broken Link Replacement

Subject: Update for your page [Page Title]

Hi [Name],

I found a broken link on your page [URL] that used to point to [dead resource]. I made an up-to-date replacement resource at [Your URL], and it would fit naturally in the same spot.

Would you be willing to replace the broken link with this newer resource?

Thanks for your time,
[Name]
[Position]
[Company]

---

## 2. Guest Post Pitch

Subject: Guest post idea for [Site Name]

Hi [Name],

I really like the content on [Site Name], especially your post on [Related Topic]. I have a guest article idea that would work well for your audience:

- Title: [Suggested Title]
- Summary: [1–2 sentence summary of the article]
- Why it fits: [Why it is relevant and valuable]

I can deliver a fully written, original draft with images and references.

If you’re interested, I’d be happy to send a draft outline.

Best regards,
[Name]
[Position]
[Company]

---
